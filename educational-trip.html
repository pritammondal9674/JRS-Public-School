Trip To 'Indian Museum' kolkata

TEACHERS: Ms. Shreoshree Ghosh, Ms. Poulami Biswas and Mr. Nirmal Biswas
CLASS: X
DATE: 28-01-2017

Description: Our students went to Indian Museum with three of our teachers. They were well guided by them. They had seen different species of mollusca, fishes, birds and many more. Then had seen the models of DNA, Geographical time scale and evolution of human. They had learnt about different varieties of animals and they came to know their origins. They had also seen the Giant Mollusca, mommies, insects and scientifically things. This trip was an educational trip for our students. They came to know about our evolutionary eras, species and organisms. They had gathered their knowledge on science. They enjoyed a lot and happy to see all these.
